A Data Analyst is a professional who collects, cleans, and interprets data to help businesses make informed decisions. They turn raw numbers into meaningful insights using tools like Excel, SQL, Python, and visualization software such as Power BI or Tableau. Data Analysts play a key role in identifying trends, solving problems, and guiding strategies for growth.
